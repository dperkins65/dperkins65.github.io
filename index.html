What to do

If you have free time, there is plenty to do in the area. Here are a few things we personally recommend.


Sightsee

  • Portland Head Lighthouse - History and a great view!
  • Portland Harbor Islands - A full day trip but there is plenty to explore.

Shop

  • The Old Port - History, shopping, and dining, all in one location.
  • Freeport - Shop the LLBean flagship store and a selection of outlet stores.

Eat

  • Petite Jacqueline - Our favorite restaurant in Portland!
  • Otto Pizza - Fantastic pizza, relaxed atmosphere.
  • Standard Baking Co - The best bagguette in world?? Perhaps. Also coffee and pastries.

Imbibe

  • Bissell Brothers Brewery - A smaller brewery that hails from the town next to where Daniel grew up, Bissell Brothers has quickly established a cult following.
  • Austin Street Brewery - On the other side of the building from Bissell Brothers, Austin Street is well worth the stop.
  • Allagash Brewery - They produce more than just Allagash White. Go find out for yourself!
  • Rising Tide Brewing Company - Closer to downtown, but no less tasty.
  • Novare Res Bier Cafe - Food and an impressive beer list. Best described as "overwhelming".
